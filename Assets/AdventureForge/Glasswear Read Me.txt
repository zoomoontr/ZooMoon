Hi, and thank you for purchasing this package!

The theme for this package is glasswear. At the moment it is split up in 3 different categoris: 
Cabinets, Household, and Chemistry.

This package contains 65 different unique models (fbx). The models named "Cabinet" and "Cabinet Lit" 
has two different metal specularity textures, one clean glass specularity and one glass specularity texture with fingerprints, making 
it 80 different prefabs of the cabinet models. There is also 2 different emission maps for the lights on the cabinets with round and square LED,
as well as an emission map for the glass plates in the cabinet to make the fingerprints glow.


In total this package consist of 125 different prefabs.
